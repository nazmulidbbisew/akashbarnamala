    • Akash's Barnamala Computer Institute is a premier computer education institute. ABCI is a pioneer in providing career-orientated courses to aspiring professionals since 1998. The institute has trained 10 thousands students through a education centre located in Munshiganj, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

    To Lead an International Standard Professional Training on Computer Study and IT “Akash’s Barnamala Computer Institute” has started its activities since 1997 about 18 years. Now “Akash's Barnamala Computer Institute” is a famous name of professional Training Institute in Munshiganj, Bangladesh. This Institute is licensed under Local Government of Bangladesh “Rampal Union Council” about Local Business.

    “Akash's Barnamala Computer Institute” is located in Shipahepara, Munshiganj, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Munshiganj District is far from Dhaka 27 K.M. and Shipahepara “Akash's Barnamala Computer Institute” is far from about 4 K.M.
